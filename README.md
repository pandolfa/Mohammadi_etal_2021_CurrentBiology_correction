Correction to:  
Mohammadi, Yang, Harpak et al. 2021 Concerted evolution reveals co-adapted 
amino acid substitutions in Na+,K+-ATPase of frogs that prey on toxic toads. 
Current Biology 31:1–9. 
https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cub.2021.03.089 

During the course of an investigation into lab records, it was discovered that one of 
the authors intentionally manually altered enzyme activity absorbance readings prior 
to our statistical analysis of the data. We carried oout a reanalysis of the data 
based on files corresponding to the original raw absorbance readings. An additional error 
that was detected resulted in all IC50 values to be overestimated by a factor of five. 
Fortunately, the re-analysis of the data revealed that all the conclusions of the paper 
remain unchanged. This respository hosts corrected supplementary materials and a PDF 
documenting all changes relative to the original aricle.
